Our Work

We work in partnership with our clients to help them achieve specific business objectives through the provision of quality IT and business solutions.

We Recruit

We recruit, train, and hire graduates, ex-forces personnel and those looking to return to work after a career break.

We Train

We provide award-winning training to equip individuals with relevant technical skills and commercial experience. In return for the training, our consultants go on to work for us for a minimum of two years which ensures continuity of service for clients

We Deploy

With flexible contract terms, FDM can provide scalable resources at short notice and across multiple client sites. We continue to support our consultants whilst they are on client site, as well as offer professional and technical support to underpin the training they recieve with FDM.

We Build Your Talent Pipeline

Clients can transfer their on-site FDM consultants to be part of their permanent teams after our agredd contractual time frames have lapses. This enables our clients to retain knowledge and skills whilst adding a broad mix of experienced professionals to their own talent pool.
